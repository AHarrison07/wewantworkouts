Let Us Help You With Your Nutrition Needs!

A critical part of any lifestyle is your diet! Whether you're looking to maintain your weight, trim down, or bulk up, the best way addition to any workout is a supportive diet.

We know you've seen nutrition labels on the food you purchase, and those give you a nice breakdown of vitamins and minerals that are found in that food, but the backbone of a good diet is proper macronutrients.

What Are Macronutrients?

Macronutrients are, by definition, a substance needed in large quantities for your body's growth and developement! While that list of vitamins on a nutrition label might seem like a lot to be ingesting, they're nothing compared to your macronutrients. Not sure what all of your main macronutrients are, or how much you should be getting of each one every day? No need to worry, we've got it all arranged in a table for you!

Macronutrients
Macronutrients Suggested Intake for Men Suggested Intake for Women
Water 3.7L 2.7L
Carbohydrates 130g 130g
Protein 56g 46g
Fiber 38g 25g
Fat 20 - 35% of calories 20 - 35% of calories
Linoleic Acid 17g 12g
Alpha-Linoleic Acid 1.6g 1.1g
Cholesterol 300mg 300mg
Trans Fatty Acids As little as possible As little as possible
Saturated Fatty Acids As little as possible As little as possible
Added Sugar No more than 25% of calories No more than 25% of calories
Dietary Reference Intakes (DRIs): Recommended Intakes for Individuals, Food and Nutrition Board, Institute of Medicine, National Academies, 2004, retrieved 2009-06-09.

Remember to consult your doctor before making any drastic changes to your diet and lifestyle! While we love giving you information about how to live a healthy and active lifestyle, nobody will be able to give you personalized information and suggestions like your doctor. The numbers in this table may be the general suggested amounts for adults aged 19 - 70 years, but that doesn't mean that they're the ideal choice for yourself. Be careful, be safe, and be informed!

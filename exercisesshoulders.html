The Kettle Bell Snatch

Looking for a workout that's dynamic and can be tailored to target multiple muscle groups? Look no further because the kettle bell snatch is here! While this exercise mainly targets the shoulders, it incorporates your legs and back, making it an effecient exercise!



How to achieve a kettle bell snatch:

  • Gripping the kettle bell with one hand, place kettle bell between your feet
  • Bend your knees and move your butt back
  • Looking straight ahead, swing the kettle bell back while keeping it between your legs
  • After the kettle bell moves backwards, immediate reverse the derection and push forward with your hips and knees to accelerate the kettle bell in an upward motion
  • Use momentum to finish the motion by locking the kettle bell overhead with a straight arm
You can perform this exercise with one hand grasping a single kettle bell, like what's shown in the image above, or you can hold one kettle bell in each hand like what's shown in our video example.

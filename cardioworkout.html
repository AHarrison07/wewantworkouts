Cardio Workout Routines

Cardio workouts have been shown to aid in weight loss, increasing bone density, reducing stress, and strenghtening your cardiovascular system. We think cardio is great, so we've assembled a couple workout outlines for you to use and customize!


Basic Cardio Workout
Warm-Up HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training) Cool-Down
Low-intensity cardio HIIT on Stair Climber Low-intensity cardio
5 minutes on treadmill 10 sets of 60 seconds high intensity/30 seconds moderate intensity on Stair Climber 5 minutes on treadmill
The outline for this workout was found at bodybuilding.com



Outdoor Cardio Workout
Warm-Up HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training) Phase 1 HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training) Phase 2 Cool-Down
Low-intensity cardio Hill sprints Dash Low-intensity cardio
5 minutes dynamic stretching or jogging 10 sets of sprinting uphill and walking back down 10 sets of 40-yard dashes followed by walking back 5 minutes of light walking or static stretching
The outline for this workout was found at bodybuilding.com



Don't forget to stretch before any exercise!

Workout of the Week!

This week, we're focusing on full body strength workouts, and we're confident that you'll enjoy the kettle bell snatch as much as we have!

The kettle bell snatch works a whole range of muscles, including the hamstrings, quads, back, and shoulders. Combining the motions of a squat with the upward swing of a kettle bell will have you feeling every muscle in your body the next day!

Doctors in the Knoxville Area

Do you live in Knoxville, TN or the surrounding area? Are you looking for a trusted nutritionist or healthcare professional? We've got two of our users' favorites right here for you!

Dr. Meijer is a local dietician who has worked with many WWW! users, and specializes in helping his patients meet their health and fitness goals! He has portable offices set up under multiple I-40 overpasses and he does house calls. Dr. Meijer doesn't have a personal phone number, but if you call the Wal Mart in Turkey Creek at 865-777-5171and ask for Stephen in the deli, he usually knows where Dr. Meijer is.


Gretchen and Finn are two up and coming healthcare professionals in Knoxville who have gained a reputation for their expert guidance in reaching health and fitness goals. Gretchen and Finn practice out of the playground at Hardin Valley Middle School, and can best be reached to make an appointment through their dad, Hans at 865-865-8658

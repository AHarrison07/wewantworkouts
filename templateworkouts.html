Foodie Corner!

An important part of any lifestyle is diet, and we love seeing what you eat to look and feel your best! This week, we're showcasing Mikhaila's homemade borscht.

Mikhaila says the secret to a perfect bowl of borscht is to go to your mom's house when she's making her own, take about three tupperware containers home with you, and store the borscht in air-tight containers until you're hungry for leftovers!

Beets, the main ingredient in borscht, contain important minerals like potassium and manganese. They also are a good source of vitamin c and fiber!
